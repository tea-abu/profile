自我介绍 / About Me

我是阿布，出生并成长于云南西双版纳勐海县，这是一个拥有深厚茶文化的地方。茶是我生命中不可或缺的一部分。从小便跟随家人上山采茶，学习制茶工艺，从采摘、杀青、揉捻、晒干，每一个步骤我都亲力亲为，充满敬畏之心。成年后我选择将这份热爱延续，深入古树茶领域，专注于原生态普洱茶的推广。

我的愿景是通过分享，让更多人理解什么是真正的古树茶，它的自然之味、文化之魂、与土地的深度连接。我希望把最纯粹的茶带到更多朋友的杯中。

I'm Abu, born and raised in Menghai County, Xishuangbanna, Yunnan – a place rich in tea culture. Tea is an inseparable part of my life. From childhood, I followed my family into the mountains to pick tea and learn the art of making it. I’ve done every step by hand – plucking, pan-firing, rolling, and sun-drying – with deep respect. As an adult, I dedicated myself to ancient tree tea and the promotion of ecological Pu’er.

My mission is to share and let more people truly understand what ancient tree tea is – its pure taste, cultural soul, and deep connection to the land. I aim to bring the most authentic tea into every friend’s cup.
